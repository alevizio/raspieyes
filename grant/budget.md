# The Regulars — Budget

Total grant award: **$1,500**. The numbers below are sized so the grant
covers essentially all hard material costs for the finished piece, with
the artist contributing labor. This ratio (grant = materials, artist =
labor) is the pattern panels consistently reward in micro-grant review.

| Category | Line items | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| **Displays** | 2× Waveshare 5" 1080×1080 round HDMI panels · 10× GC9A01 1.28" SPI round displays (240×240) · 2× spare GC9A01 | **$360** |
| **Cameras** | 2× Raspberry Pi Camera Module 3 Wide · 2× 500 mm CSI-2 ribbon cables · 22-pin → 15-pin adapters | **$90** |
| **Enclosure** | Laser-cut 12 mm black-stained birch plywood face · 18 mm poplar box sides · router bits for roundover cutouts · wood stain, sealer | **$200** |
| **Electronics** | 5 V 10 A PSU · 5 V → 3.3 V buck converter (2 A) · perfboard fan-out hub · ribbon cable, Dupont kit, 28 AWG hookup wire · M2.5 standoff set · 2× 40 mm 5 V fans + dust filter | **$130** |
| **Mounting & protection** | 3 mm non-glare acrylic front · French cleat rated ≥10 kg · anti-theft lanyard hardware · IEC kettle-lead pass-through with strain relief | **$150** |
| **Contingency / spares** | Extra GC9A01 beyond the two above, one spare CSI camera, backup PSU | **$170** |
| **Artist labor** | ~40 hours design, fabrication, assembly, finishing, install/de-install (intentionally under-priced — signals the grant funds materials, not fee) | **$400** |
| **Total** | | **$1,500** |

## Notes for the judges

- The Raspberry Pi 5 that drives the piece is **not** on this budget;
  it is already owned. If it were included, the total would still
  fall inside the grant.
- No recurring costs: no cloud services, no subscriptions, no data
  plan. The piece plugs into one wall outlet at Madrone and runs
  indefinitely.
- Because the work sells commission-free per the grant terms, any
  sale during the show goes directly back into materials for future
  pieces in this series.
- Install and de-install are performed by the artist in person. The
  venue is asked only to provide wall access and one standard outlet.
